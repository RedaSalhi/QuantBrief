Sample Content Structure

A glimpse into our weekly LaTeX-formatted newsletter

Executive Summary (1 minutes)

Concise overview of the week's dominant theme with the most impactful market moves, macro headlines, and one-sentence forward guidance. Includes volatility spikes, index returns, and sector highlights.

Cross-Asset Market Overview (2 minute)

Performance of equities, credit, rates, FX, and commodities with contextualization. Covers S&P 500, global indices, VIX, Treasury spreads, DXY, and key correlations.

Research Review (2 minutes)

Summary and critical analysis of 2‚Äì3 recent academic papers from SSRN, arXiv, or NBER. Includes key findings, model overviews, implementation insights, and how they relate to current markets.

Corporate & Earnings Focus (1 minute)

Key earnings results, M&A activity, strategic announcements, and valuation breakdowns. Emphasizes margin surprises, management guidance, and broader sector impact.

Macro & Policy Outlooks (30 seconds)

Quick summary of central bank decisions, inflation expectations, rate hike probabilities, and key macro data releases that may influence asset pricing in the upcoming week.

Outlook & Catalysts (1 minute)

Forward calendar of economic events (e.g., CPI, FOMC), earnings clusters, and technical setups. Includes potential tail-risk scenarios and market sensitivity triggers.
